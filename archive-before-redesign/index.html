Abstract

I am a Creative Designer based in New York. It all started when I was little. Both of my parents are artists. They brought me to travel the world with brushes and paint. However, I never thought I would study art one day until I took a graphic design class in my sophomore year of high school. This class changed everything I know about art and made me start to fall in love with digital media. Later, I learned photography, animation made the X Rounds, 3D modeling made the Light Up, and AIGC, and tried all the new media I had not used. I tried to follow the trend of new media, and hopefully one day I can lead the trend.
