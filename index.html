01

Background

My parents are artists, so I grew up around travel, brushes, paint, and visual observation. A graphic design class in high school shifted that foundation toward digital media and made design feel like a language I could keep expanding.

02

Process

I move between hand-made sensitivity and computational tools. My workflow often starts with a visual atmosphere, then tests it through code, layout, motion, and small interactions.

03

Direction

I am interested in interfaces that feel calm but precise: minimal surfaces, responsive composition, and technology that supports creative attention instead of overwhelming it.
