Creative Direction

From digital media to spatial code.

I am a Creative Designer based in New York. It all started when I was little. Both of my parents are artists, and they brought me to travel the world with brushes and paint.

A graphic design class in high school changed how I understood art and made me fall in love with digital media. Later, I explored photography, animation, 3D modeling, and AIGC, following new media tools while building my own design language.

Spatial Canvas System

Primitive shapes, motion, interaction, and depth.

This page keeps the course tutorial requirements while presenting the sketches as a polished computational design study. The p5.js section focuses on 2D primitive drawing and interaction. The Three.js section translates the same soft geometric language into 3D space.

01 / P5.js

Static Study

A static 2D drawing made with rectangles, ellipses, lines, and triangles. The composition studies balance, depth, and quiet color through primitive shapes.

02 / P5.js

Interactive Field

An animated drawing that responds to the mouse. Move across the canvas to bend the field; click inside the canvas to pause or restart the motion.

03 / Three.js

Primitive Model

A beginner Three.js scene with camera, renderer, lights, grid, a rotating primitive mesh, and an animation loop.

04 / Three.js

Orbit Study

An interactive 3D scene with primitive geometry and OrbitControls. Drag the canvas to orbit around the forms.

05 / Three.js

Atmospheric Geometry

A fog-based scene with transparent primitives and layered depth, extending the tutorial structure into a softer spatial mood.
